How it works

Three steps. One clean monthly cycle.

Step 01

You send the inputs.

Each month, you send a defined set of source data: actuals, revenue detail, headcount updates, and the operating inputs needed to refresh the model.

Step 02

We refresh the model and reporting.

We integrate actuals, refresh the forecast by revenue component, update the variance analysis, and refresh the reporting layer.

Step 03

We meet, review, and close.

One meeting. We walk through what changed, what matters, and what needs attention. Then the cycle is done until next month.

Your time commitment is the inputs. The rest happens on our end — with the same person on it every month.
